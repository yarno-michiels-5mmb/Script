Born in the Netherlands, professional swim suit model and full time superstar Bizzey took the world by storm as a part of the legendary Yellow Claw. In 2017 he decided to pursue a solo career path in order to be able to spend more time with his family back home. This sparked a year filled with golden & platinum releases and collaborations with Dutch rap heavyweights such as Frenna, Yung Felix, Josylvio, LouiVos and Mula B. Bizzey's characteristic sound contains elements of hip hop, R&B, Baile Funk, Reggaeton and club music of all types.
